About Sonny's Indian Kitchen

Authentic Indian Cuisine in Chatham

Founded in 2010, Sonny's Indian Kitchen has been serving authentic Indian cuisine to the Chatham community and beyond. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring you experience the true flavors of India.

We use only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods to bring you dishes that are rich in flavor and aroma. From our tandoori specialties to our slow-cooked curries, every dish tells a story of India's diverse culinary heritage.

"At Sonny's, we believe food is more than sustenance - it's an experience that brings people together and creates lasting memories."
